STATEMENT OF OUR MISSION:

As members of God's family we are called to be a Christ centered community where the Good News is joyfully shared.

We affirm the worth of all persons as God's children and commit ourselves to live in Christ's love and respond in obedience to Christ's call.

STATEMENT OF OUR CORE VALUES:

  • A Christ Centered church that is biblically grounded
  • Prayer as the foundation for our personal and congregational life
  • Worship that brings us into the presence of God and energizes us to action
  • A passion to minister to the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of all God's people
  • A community where all are heard, loved, and valued as children of the King
  • Authenticity in relating to one another within and beyond our faith community
  • Authenticity of the Spirit's life-changing work in the individual and congregation
